# SmartPayroll HR Policy: Annual Leave — Spain

**Document ID:** HR-POL-ES-001
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Country:** Spain (ES)

## 1. Statutory Entitlement

Under Article 38 of the Spanish Workers' Statute (Estatuto de
los Trabajadores), all full-time employees are entitled to a
minimum of 22 working days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

Part-time employees receive annual leave proportional to their
working hours.

## 2. Leave Year

The leave year runs from January 1 to December 31. Leave cannot
be carried forward to the following year unless agreed in writing.

## 3. Notice Requirements

- Leave of 1-3 days: 5 working days advance notice
- Leave of 4-10 days: 10 working days advance notice
- Leave greater than 10 days: 15 working days advance notice

## 4. Approval Process

All annual leave requests must be submitted via the HR system.
Requests are reviewed by the direct manager within 5 working days.

## 5. Public Holidays

Spanish national public holidays (14 per year) are additional
to annual leave entitlement.

## 6. Interaction with Sick Leave

If an employee falls ill during approved annual leave and provides
a medical certificate within 3 days, the days may be reclassified
as sick leave.

## 7. Termination

On termination, any accrued but untaken annual leave is paid out
at the employee's daily rate.
