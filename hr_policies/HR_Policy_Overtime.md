# SmartPayroll HR Policy: Overtime — General Guidelines

**Document ID:** HR-POL-GEN-002
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Scope:** All SmartPayroll locations

## 1. Definition

Overtime is any working time beyond the employee's contractual
hours per week. Standard working hours at SmartPayroll: 38 hours
per week (EU operations).

## 2. Authorisation

All overtime must be pre-authorised by the employee's direct
manager. Unauthorised overtime will not be compensated.

## 3. Compensation

Overtime is compensated by one of two methods:
- Time off in lieu (TOIL): 1.25 hours off per overtime hour
- Financial: 125% of hourly rate for first 8 hours per month,
  150% for hours beyond that

## 4. Impact on Attrition

SmartPayroll data shows employees working regular overtime (more than
5 hours per week) have significantly higher probability of
voluntary resignation. Managers should monitor overtime patterns
and discuss workload proactively in 1:1 meetings.

## 5. Maximum Hours

EU Working Time Directive: maximum 48 hours per week averaged
over 17 weeks. No employee may exceed this limit.

## 6. Recording

All overtime must be recorded in the HR system within 48 hours
of completion. Unrecorded overtime cannot be compensated.
